
rclone config failed on the replica. Common causes:
  * a regular Yandex account password was used instead of
    an APP-PASSWORD. Yandex WebDAV accepts only app-
    passwords from https://id.yandex.ru/security/app-passwords
    (even on accounts without 2FA).
  * the app-password was created for a category other than
    "Files" (Файлы / WebDAV) — e.g. "Mail" tokens are
    scoped differently and the WebDAV endpoint rejects them.
  * the login was a display name or email (e.g. "Joe Doe",
    "joe@yandex.ru") instead of the bare Yandex login.
  * the app-password was mistyped (it is usually shown as
    4 blocks of 4 letters; paste WITHOUT spaces).
  * the replica cannot reach https://webdav.yandex.ru.

The partially-created 'yandex:' remote has been rolled
back on VM2.
Re-run `inv setup-replica` to try again; it will
prompt for credentials fresh.
